Presentaur

With Presentaur you only need
one computer and a phone
to control the flow of

Login To Get Started


Presentaur allows the flow of multiple presentations to be controlled from a single device


1. Login to create a new Presentaur
2. You will be given a link for your presenters to signup at
3. Once everyone has signed up start your presentaur
4. DJ the presentaur from another computer or your phone
